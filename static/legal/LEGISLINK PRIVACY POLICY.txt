LEGISLINK PRIVACY POLICY
Effective Date: 01/19/2026

This Privacy Policy describes how LegisLink Ltd. Co., a Utah limited liability company (“LegisLink,” “we,” “us,” or “our”), collects, uses, and protects information obtained through the LegisLink platform (“Platform”).

By creating an account and checking the acceptance box during signup, you consent to this Privacy Policy.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
We may collect:
- Account information such as name, email address, and organization
- Legislative tracking data, bill selections, notes, and annotations
- Usage data related to how you interact with the Platform
- Communications you send to LegisLink

2. HOW WE USE INFORMATION
We use collected information to:
- Operate and maintain the Platform
- Provide legislative tracking, summaries, and alerts
- Improve Platform functionality and services
- Develop enhanced legislative analysis and features
- Communicate with users regarding accounts or updates

3. NO DISCLOSURE OF CONTACT INFORMATION
LegisLink does not sell, rent, or disclose user contact information to any third parties.

We do not share personally identifiable contact information for marketing or commercial purposes.

4. USE OF PLATFORM DATA
Information entered into the Platform may be:
- Used internally to improve services
- Aggregated and anonymized for analytics and feature development
- Incorporated into legislative summaries and analytical tools

No personally identifiable contact information will be publicly disclosed.

5. LEGAL REQUIREMENTS
Information may be disclosed if required by law, court order, or valid legal process under Utah or federal law.

6. DATA SECURITY
LegisLink employs reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information. However, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

7. DATA RETENTION
Information is retained as long as necessary to provide the Platform, comply with legal obligations, and enforce agreements.

8. USER RIGHTS
You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your account information, subject to applicable legal requirements.

9. CHILDREN’S PRIVACY
The Platform is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. LegisLink does not knowingly collect information from minors.
